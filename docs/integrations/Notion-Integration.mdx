Here's how to set up Granola to save notes to Notion:


<Steps>
  <Step >
  Open your settings via your initial in the top right, then click into Notion under `Integrations` on the right.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/Notion-1.png"/>
  </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step >
  After clicking 'Connect Notion', you'll be redirected to your browser to authorize Granola. Click Next.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/Notion-2.png"/>
  </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
  Make sure to select the 'Use a template provided by the developer' option. We currently don't support selecting specific pages.
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/Notion-3.png"/>
  </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step >
  You're connected! You'll now see Notion appear as a sharing option in a Granola note.
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/Notion-5.png"/>
  </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
  When you share a note to Notion, it'll be saved to your personal Notion database. 

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/Notion-6.png"/>
  </Frame>
<br/>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Important:** You must not change the Notion database structure as this will break the integration. In order to reference the notes elsewhere in Notion, you'll want to use 'Link to page' in Notion.
</Info>
