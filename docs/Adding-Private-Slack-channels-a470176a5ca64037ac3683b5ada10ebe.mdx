---
title:  Adding Private Slack channels
description: "To post to private channels in Granola, you’ll first need to add it to the channel."
mode: wide
icon: hashtag
iconType: solid
---

1. Head to the channel in Slack
2. Type `/` in the new message box
3. Click `add apps to channel`
4. Head back to Granola, and search for your new channel in the ‘post to slack’ dialog
