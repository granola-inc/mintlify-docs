So you’ve just downloaded **Granola**—awesome! Let’s get you set up on macOS.

## 1. Download the Granola .dmg

You can find the latest release on our [website](https://www.granola.ai).
Click **Download Granola for Mac**. Once it’s finished downloading, open your **Downloads** folder.

<Note>
  If you have trouble finding your .dmg file, press <kbd>cmd + Space</kbd> 
  to open Spotlight and search for "Granola.dmg".
</Note>

## 2. Drag Granola to Applications

Open `Granola.dmg`, then drag the **Granola** icon over to your **Applications** folder.

![Dragging the Granola icon to Applications](/images/screenshots/install-granola.png)

## 3. Accept Permissions

Double-click **Granola** in your Applications folder. You’ll see a prompt that says 
“Granola is an app downloaded from the internet. Are you sure you want to open it?”

Click **Open**.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Common Installation Issues">
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Security Settings:</strong> If your Mac says it cannot verify the developer, 
      go to System Settings &gt; Privacy & Security and click "Open Anyway".</li>
      <li><strong>Unexpected Crash:</strong> Turn off any VPN or firewall that might
      block Granola from downloading required files.</li>
      <li><strong>Mac OS not up to date</strong> Make sure your Mac OS is up to date so Granola can run.</li>
    </ul>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

**That’s it!** You’re now ready to run Granola for the first time. After installation, 
the app will guide you through signing in and integrating your calendar.
